Tidewell Plus

Everything, without limits.

Four capabilities designed around the moments that matter most on a GLP-1 — built to protect your supply, your appointments, and your sense of progress.

Dose & Supply Guardian

Your supply, guarded.

These medications are expensive, hard to replace, and often short in supply. Guardian tracks every pen, counts what's left, and flags your refill window before it becomes a problem.

Pen inventory — units remaining, automatically counted
Refill timing — flagged days before you'd run out, not after
Dose escalation — your step-up schedule, tracked against your log
Doctor Visit Briefing

Your 12 minutes, prepared.

Appointments are short, and prescribers work from what you tell them. Briefing compiles your check-ins, weight trend, side effects, and missed doses into a clean summary you can share or pull up the moment you sit down.

Auto-compiled — built from your logs, no manual notes
One-tap sharing — send ahead, or show it in the room
Always current — updates with every check-in
Private Journey Record

You've come further than you think.

Progress on GLP-1s is gradual — easy to dismiss day to day. Your Journey Record keeps a quiet chronology of every milestone since day one. Scroll back and see what has actually shifted.
