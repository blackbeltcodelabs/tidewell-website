What Tidewell does

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built around the real rhythms of life on a GLP-1 — not around what an app thinks your health journey should look like.

Shot & Dose Tracking

Select your medication, choose your dose, and tap your injection site on the body diagram. Tidewell remembers your last site so you don't double up.

Daily Check-In

A 20-second ritual. Rate your mood, energy, and food noise — that quiet background craving that GLP-1s tend to quiet. Note any side effects. Then go.

Body & Weight Trends

Log your weight and waist over time. Gentle trend lines — blue for weight, amber for waist — show your shape without the obsession. BMI shown if you want it.

The Learn Library

Short companion pieces — The Mind, The Body, Living With It — covering what GLP-1s actually do. Written for humans, not clinicians. New reads unlock as you go.

Apple Health Sync

Reads your steps, sleep, and heart rate from Apple Health to give your daily check-in more context — automatically, with your permission.

Tidewell Plus

Export your records as PDF or CSV for your prescriber. Unlock all companion pieces, get personalized nudges, and keep your full history synced across devices.

The story

Why I built this.

I built Tidewell because I couldn't find an app that understood what it actually feels like to live on a GLP-1.

The research is clear: there's a meaningful gap between what these medications do to the body and how the mind catches up with the changes. Most apps want to count calories or gamify your progress. But that's not what this moment calls for.

Tidewell is a small attempt to fill that gap — quietly, without judgment. A 20-second check-in ritual for the week between shots. A gentle way to notice what's shifting, without making it a project.

Thank you for being here early. It means a lot.
