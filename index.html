The story

Why I built this.

I built Tidewell because I couldn't find an app that understood what it actually feels like to live on a GLP-1.

The research is clear: there's a meaningful gap between what these medications do to the body and how the mind catches up with the changes. Most apps want to count calories or gamify your progress. But that's not what this moment calls for.

Tidewell is a small attempt to fill that gap — quietly, without judgment. A 20-second check-in ritual for the week between shots. A gentle way to notice what's shifting, without making it a project.

Thank you for being here early. It means a lot.
