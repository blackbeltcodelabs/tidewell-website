Medical Disclaimer

Effective date: June 24, 2026

Tidewell is not a medical device

Tidewell is a personal wellness companion application. It is designed to help users track their GLP-1 medication schedule, log daily wellbeing check-ins, and observe personal patterns over time. Tidewell is not classified as a medical device under any regulatory framework, and it has not been evaluated, cleared, or approved by the U.S. Food & Drug Administration (FDA), the Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA), or any other regulatory body.

Not a substitute for professional medical advice

Nothing in the Tidewell app, including its features, content, data displays, learn library articles, or any other materials, constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. The information provided is for general informational and personal wellness purposes only.

Always seek the advice of a qualified healthcare professional — such as your physician, endocrinologist, dietitian, or pharmacist — regarding your specific medical conditions, medications, and treatment decisions. Do not disregard, delay seeking, or discontinue professional medical advice because of something you have read or observed in Tidewell.

GLP-1 medications and your healthcare provider

GLP-1 receptor agonist medications (such as semaglutide, tirzepatide, and others) are prescription drugs that must be managed in consultation with a licensed healthcare provider. Tidewell does not prescribe, adjust, or recommend dosing for any medication. Any questions about your medication — including dose changes, side effects, missed injections, or stopping treatment — must be directed to your prescribing provider or pharmacist.

Patterns are not diagnoses

Tidewell helps you notice patterns in how you feel over time. These observations are personal data points — they are not clinical measurements, diagnostic outputs, or medical findings. A change in a trend or a pattern you observe in Tidewell is not a diagnosis and should not be treated as one. If you are concerned about a symptom, trend, or change in how you feel, consult a healthcare professional.

Learn Library content

The articles and educational content within the Tidewell Learn Library are written to be broadly informative about GLP-1 medications and their known effects. This content is based on published research and is intended for general educational purposes. It is not tailored to your individual health circumstances, medical history, or current treatment plan. It does not represent the position of any medical institution, and it should not replace the advice of your healthcare provider.

In an emergency

If you are experiencing a medical emergency — including but not limited to severe allergic reactions, chest pain, difficulty breathing, signs of stroke, or thoughts of self-harm — stop using this app and contact emergency services immediately by calling your local emergency number (such as 000 in Australia, 911 in the United States, or 999 in the United Kingdom).

Tidewell is not an emergency service. It does not monitor for emergencies, alert caregivers, or provide crisis support.

Mental health

If you are struggling with your mental health, body image, or relationship with food or medication, please reach out to a qualified mental health professional. Tidewell is designed to reduce shame and pressure — not to replace compassionate human support. Helpful resources include your GP, a licensed therapist, or a mental health helpline in your country.

Apple Health data

When you grant Tidewell access to Apple Health (HealthKit), data such as steps, sleep, and heart rate may be displayed within the app to add context to your check-ins. These readings are sourced from your device sensors or connected apps and are not clinical measurements. They should not be used to make medical decisions without professional guidance.

No warranties on health outcomes

We make no representations, warranties, or guarantees — express or implied — that using Tidewell will produce any specific health outcome, weight loss result, or improvement in your wellbeing. Individual results vary. Tidewell is a tool to support self-awareness, not a programme for medical intervention.
