What people search

Does Mounjaro® cause side effects like nausea or fatigue?

Nausea, fatigue, and digestive changes are commonly reported when starting Mounjaro® or increasing a dose. Most people find these ease over the first several weeks as the body adjusts. If a side effect feels severe or doesn't improve, that's worth raising with your prescriber.

Why does my appetite change throughout the week?

Many people on Mounjaro® notice their appetite and food noise shift across the dosing week — steadier in the days right after a shot, sometimes more present as the week goes on. This is a commonly reported pattern, not a sign anything is wrong.

Where should I inject Mounjaro®?

Mounjaro® is typically injected once weekly into the abdomen, thigh, or upper arm, with sites rotated to reduce irritation. Always follow the specific instructions provided with your prescription and ask your prescriber or pharmacist if you're unsure.

Is Mounjaro® the same as Zepbound®?

Mounjaro® and Zepbound® both contain the same active ingredient, tirzepatide, but are approved for different uses. Your prescriber can explain which applies to your situation.
