Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

Tidewell is provided by Tara Cloud Retail, doing business as Black Belt Code Labs ("we", "us", "our"), owned and operated by Rahul Bhattacharya. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Tidewell mobile application and website (tidewellapp.com).

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you use Tidewell, you may provide the following information:

  • Daily check-in responses (mood, energy, hunger, side effects)
  • Injection logs (date, dose, injection site)
  • Body weight and measurement entries
  • Account information (email address, if you create an account)

Information from Apple Health (HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, Tidewell may read health data from Apple Health, including steps, sleep data, and heart rate. This data is used only to provide context within your Tidewell experience. We do not upload HealthKit data to our servers. It is processed entirely on your device and never shared with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes.

Technical information

We may collect standard technical information to operate and improve the app, including:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions (anonymised)
  • Crash reports and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Tidewell app
  • Display your trends, patterns, and check-in history
  • Sync your data securely across your devices
  • Improve app features and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send you important notices about the app (not marketing without your consent)

3. Data Storage

Your Tidewell account data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform that encrypts data at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS. Supabase infrastructure is hosted on AWS and complies with relevant data protection standards.

Apple Health (HealthKit) data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to Tidewell's servers.

4. Data We Do Not Sell or Share

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purpose. Your health and wellness data is yours — we collect only what is needed to deliver the app experience.

We do not share identifiable data with:

  • Advertising networks
  • Data brokers
  • Social media platforms
  • Analytics companies in a way that identifies you

5. Subscriptions and Payments

Tidewell Plus subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store your payment card information. Billing is governed by Apple's or Google's respective terms of service and privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@blackbeltcodelabs.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability of your data
  • Objection to certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@blackbeltcodelabs.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Tidewell is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or by email. Continued use of Tidewell after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
