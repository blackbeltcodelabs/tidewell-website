What people search

What's the difference between semaglutide and brand-name versions like Ozempic or Wegovy?

Semaglutide is the active ingredient; Ozempic and Wegovy are brand names containing it, approved for different uses. Compounded semaglutide is also available in some regions, often at a different price point, and is worth discussing with your prescriber regarding sourcing and oversight.

Is generic or compounded semaglutide as effective as the branded version?

This is a question best directed to your prescriber or pharmacist, since formulation, dosing, and oversight can vary between branded and compounded products.

What side effects are common with semaglutide?

Nausea, digestive changes, and fatigue are widely reported, particularly when starting or increasing a dose. Most people find these ease with time.

How should I store and handle my semaglutide injection?

Most semaglutide products require refrigeration before first use, with specific guidance on room-temperature storage afterward depending on the product. Always follow the instructions provided with your specific prescription.
