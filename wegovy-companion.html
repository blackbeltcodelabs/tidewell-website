What people search

The questions that show up mid-week.

How long does it take for Wegovy® to start working?

Many people notice appetite changes within the first days to weeks, with effects building gradually as the dose is titrated upward over time, following your prescriber's plan. Individual experience varies.

Is it normal for weight loss to slow down or plateau?

Plateaus are commonly reported during longer GLP-1 journeys and don't necessarily mean progress has stopped. Many people find the trend continues over a longer window even when a particular week or month looks flat.

What side effects should I expect when starting Wegovy®?

Nausea, digestive changes, and fatigue are commonly reported, especially around dose increases. These often ease as the body adjusts. Reach out to your prescriber about anything severe or persistent.

Can I lose muscle while losing weight on Wegovy®?

Some portion of weight lost during any significant weight loss, including on GLP-1 medications, can come from lean mass. Many people find that paying attention to protein intake and including some gentle strength activity helps. Ask your prescriber or a dietitian for guidance specific to you.
