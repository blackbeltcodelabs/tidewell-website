What people search

Is Zepbound® the same medication as Mounjaro®?

Zepbound® and Mounjaro® share the same active ingredient, tirzepatide, but are approved for different purposes. Your prescriber can explain what's relevant to your specific situation.

What does dose titration mean for Zepbound®?

Titration refers to gradually increasing the dose over time, usually to help the body adjust and to manage side effects. Your prescriber sets the specific schedule for you.

Why do I feel different in the days right after my shot versus later in the week?

Many people notice a rhythm across their dosing week — often steadier appetite and food noise in the first days, with some return of both later on. This pattern is widely reported and isn't a sign anything is wrong.

What helps with nausea on Zepbound®?

Smaller, simpler meals, staying hydrated, and eating slowly are commonly mentioned by people managing nausea on GLP-1 medications. If nausea is severe or persistent, your prescriber may have additional options.
